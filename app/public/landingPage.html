SDG 3 Health Targets

Sustainable Development Goal 3 aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. Our platform focuses on leveraging technology to accelerate progress on these key targets.

Target 3.1

Reduce maternal mortality to less than 70 per 100,000 live births

Target 3.2

End preventable deaths of newborns and children under 5 years of age

Target 3.3

End the epidemics of AIDS, tuberculosis, malaria and neglected tropical diseases

Target 3.4

Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases

Target 3.8

Achieve universal health coverage, including financial risk protection

Target 3.9

Reduce deaths and illnesses from hazardous chemicals and pollution
